About PIPS e-Learning Platform

The PIPS E-Learning Portal is an initiative of the Pakistan Institute for Parliamentary Services (PIPS) aimed at providing high-quality training and knowledge resources for parliamentarians, parliamentary staff, and governance professionals. This platform offers structured courses, expert insights, and certification opportunities to enhance professional skills in legislative processes, policymaking, and governance.

What does the LMS offer?

Structure Self-Paced Courses

Organized lessons that let you learn at your own pace.

Expert-led Traning Programs

Interactive sessions led by industry professionals.

Interactive Learning Materials

Engaging multimedia content that keeps you involved such as videos, PDFs, quizzes, etc.

Recognized Certifications for Skill Enhancement

Accredited credentials that boost your career prospects.
