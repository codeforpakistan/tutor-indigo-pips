Privacy Policy

Introduction

Purpose
This is the website for PIPS e-Learning Platform. We protect your personal information using industry-standard safeguards.
Sharing / Changes
We may share your information with your consent or as required by law, and we will always let you know when we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy.

Information we collect

Registration
When you create an account with us, we collect information (including your name and email address).
Profile Information
We collect information when you fill out our registration forms. A complete form including personal and professional details – like your gender, age, occupation, education, among others – ends up as statistics in our services.
Cookies
We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information.
Log Files
We collect information from the devices and networks that you use to access our Service.

How personal information is used

Consent
You agree that information you provide on your profile can be seen used by us as described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.
Communication
We communicate with you using email. We may send you messages relating to the availability of the Service, security, or other service-related issues.
Service Development
We use the information you provide to us to conduct analysis, research and development, and to customize your experience and try to make it relevant and useful to you.
Sharing Information
We do not share any personally identifying information that you provide to us. Non-identifying information is publically shared as part of our Service.
Third Parties
We work with developers to build applications using our developer tools. If you have given an application access to your account, you can revoke that permission anytime. Also, you can opt out of providing information to developers through your connections.
Polls and Surveys
We conduct our own surveys and polls and also help third parties do this type of research. Your participation in surveys or polls is up to you. You may also opt out of getting invitations to participate in surveys.
Compliance
We may disclose your personal information if compelled by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if necessary to enforce our User Agreement.
International Use
We may process your information outside the country where you live.

Choices & Obligations

Access / Closure
You can change your account information at any time by editing your profile, or by closing your account. You can also ask us for additional information we may have about your account.
Data Retention
We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed. We may keep certain information even after you close your account if it is necessary to comply with our legal obligations, meet regulatory requirements, resolve disputes, prevent fraud and abuse, or enforce this agreement.

Important Information

Minimum Age
You have to meet our minimum age requirements to create an account.
Marketing
We don't share any of your personal information with third parties for direct marketing.
Security
We take privacy and security seriously and have enabled HTTPS access to our site, in addition to existing SSL access over mobile devices. Also, please know that the Internet is not a secure environment, so be careful and select strong passwords.
